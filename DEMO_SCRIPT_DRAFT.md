**Total Steps**: 16
**Narrator Tone**: Professional, Helpful, Secure.

### Phase 1: Access & Security
**Step 1: The Landing Page (Hero)**
*   *Visual*: The main splash screen with "Decipher Handwritten Letters Instantly."
*   *Script*: "Welcome to the Letter Translator Gemini platform—a specialized AI tool built specifically for Children Believe to bridge the communication gap with our international sponsors."

**Step 2: Sign-In Trigger**
*   *Visual*: User clicking 'Start New Translation' and the Auth Modal popping up.
*   *Script*: "Getting started is secure and simple. All regional staff use their corporate credentials to access the workspace."

**Step 3: Adaptive Security (Failed Attempt)**
*   *Visual*: Entering a wrong password and seeing the error.
*   *Script*: "The system is built on Supabase security. If you have trouble remembering your password, the system proactively monitors attempts to protect Child Data."

**Step 4: The Recovery Link**
*   *Visual*: The 'Forgot Password' link appearing after the 2nd failed attempt.
*   *Script*: "After two attempts, a secure recovery link appears, allowing staff to reset their access without needing immediate IT intervention."

**Step 5: Password Reset Workflow**
*   *Visual*: The New Password modal.
*   *Script*: "Upon clicking the recovery email, you are guided through a secure reset process to establish new, strong credentials instantly."

**Step 6: Successful Entry**
*   *Visual*: The workspace loading for the first time.
*   *Script*: "Once authenticated, you are brought into your personal workspace, where all processing happens in a secure, private environment."

### Phase 2: The Translation Workflow
**Step 7: Uploading the Letter**
*   *Visual*: Dragging a photo of a handwritten letter into the upload zone.
*   *Script*: "Simply upload a high-resolution photo or PDF of the child's letter. The system is designed to handle messy handwriting and various paper types."

**Step 8: Image Optimization**
*   *Visual*: The loading/compression bar.
*   *Script*: "The app automatically compresses and optimizes the image to ensure the AI 'sees' every character with maximum clarity."

**Step 9: The Workspace Overview**
*   *Visual*: The letter appearing on the left, empty translation box on the right.
*   *Script*: "This side-by-side view allows you to verify the original handwriting against its digital transcription at every step."

**Step 10: Triggering the AI (Decipher)**
*   *Visual*: Clicking the purple 'Decipher Document' button.
*   *Script*: "Powered by Google Gemini 2.0 Flash, the AI begins a multimodal analysis—it doesn't just read the text; it understands the visual context."

**Step 11: Native Transcription**
*   *Visual*: The 'Original Transcription' tab filling with Amharic/Telugu/Spanish script.
*   *Script*: "First, the system provides a verbatim transcription in the native script. This is critical for archival accuracy and verification."

**Step 12: High-Fidelity English Translation**
*   *Visual*: Switching to the 'English Translation' tab.
*   *Script*: "Next, the system delivers a literal English translation. Unlike generic AI, ours is trained on 'Golden References' to ensure regional dialects are respected."

**Step 13: Accuracy Audit (Zooming in)**
*   *Visual*: Pointing out a specific detail (e.g., a child's name or a mention of a goat).
*   *Script*: "Notice the accuracy: the AI identifies specific names and cultural markers that standard translation tools often hallucinate or miss."
