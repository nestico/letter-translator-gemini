# Final Demo Voiceover Script: Letter Translator Gemini

**Objective**: A professional walkthrough for Children Believe stakeholders, focusing on security, accuracy, and the AI learning loop.

---

| Phase | Visual Action | Professional Narration |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Intro** | User clicks the "Sign In" button on the landing page. | "Welcome to Letter Translator Gemini. This platform is designed to bridge the gap between regional staff and international sponsors through secure, AI-powered letter processing." |
| **Security** | Entering email and password. Show recovery link if possible. | "Staff enter their corporate credentials to access the workspace. For added security, an adaptive recovery flow is triggered after two failed attempts, allowing authorized users to regain access securely." |
| **Upload** | Clicking "Start New Translation" and "Select from Gallery." | "To begin, we start a new translation by selecting child letter images from our regional gallery. The system supports up to three images per letter to handle multi-page correspondence." |
| **Queue** | Reviewing image order and clicking "Translate Document." | "Before processing, we confirm the images are in the correct sequence. This ensures the narrative of the child's story flows perfectly from first page to last." |
| **Processing** | Selecting language and clicking "Decipher Document with AI." | "By selecting the source language, we trigger the Gemini 2.0 Flash engine. The model doesn't just read the text; it performs a multimodal analysis to capture the authentic voice of the child." |
| **Auditing** | Showing side-by-side view; clicking "Edit Text." | "The native transcription and English translation are displayed side-by-side. Our staff can instantly edit the text to ensure cultural nuances and regional dialects are captured with 100% fidelity." |
| **Branding** | Entering name and Child ID in Export box; clicking Export. | "Exporting is professional and branded. By entering the auditor's name and the Child ID, we generate a secure document ready for delivery to international sponsors." |
| **Final PDF** | Opening the PDF to show the images, text, and metadata. | "The final PDF preserves the original handwriting alongside the verified English translation and the Child ID, creating a complete and transparent record of the communication." |
| **The Loop** | Clicking "Approve & Save" and navigating to "History." | "After verification, we approve and save the translation to our regional history. This maintains a searchable digital archive for our office." |
| **Innovation** | Clicking the 'Star' icon to set as a "Golden Translation." | "To ensure continuous improvement, we can mark accurate records as 'Golden Translations.' This allows the AI model to learn from our best work, improving the accuracy of all future translations across the organization." |

---

### Instructions for Audio Recording:
1. **Pace**: Moderate and steady. Pause for 1 second between rows to allow for video transitions.
2. **Emphasis**: Put a professional emphasis on *"literal fidelity"*, *"corporate credentials"*, and *"Golden Translations"*.
3. **Closing**: End with a helpful, encouraging tone.
